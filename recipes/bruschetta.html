Description

Bruschetta

A simple Italian dish with fried bread and a fresh tomato & herb mix

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Fresh bread
  • Black pepper
  • Sea salt
  • Basil
  • Olive oil
  • Onion powder

Steps

  1. Chop the tomatoes and add into a bowl with the spices and olive oil
  2. Lightly fry the sliced bread until golden
  3. Add tomato mix onto the bread & serve
