Description

Jamaican Rice & Peas

A simple dish typically served as a side. Rice & Peas is a staple of Caribbean cooking. It uses Coconut milk and thyme to create an enticing aroma.

Ingredients

  • Rice (Basmati/Long grain)
  • Canned coconut milk
  • Fresh Thyme
  • Gungo peas or kidney beans
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Optional: scotch bonnet pepper

Steps

  1. Lightly fry the onion & garlic until fragrant
  2. Wash rice thorougly and place into a pan with water and coconut milk
  3. Add thyme, peas, garlic, onion and the scotch bonnet and allow to simmer until the rice is cooked
  4. Let stand and serve
