Description

Pulled pork

A rustic dish originating in the American south. This slow cooked meat is packed full of flavour and guaranteed to please at any barbecue.

Ingredients

  • Pork shoulder joint (bone out)
  • Paprika
  • Black pepper
  • Sea salt
  • Chilli powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Brown sugar

Steps

  1. Combine all of the spices & brown sugar in a bowl
  2. Rub the spice blend into every part of the pork shoulder, don't be afraid to get physical
  3. Place pork joint in a slow cooker - or better yet a smoker - and let cook for a minimu of 8 hours
  4. Let stand and serve
